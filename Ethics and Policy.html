Ethics and Policy

1. Introduction

This Ethics and Policy document outlines the standards and guidelines that govern the conduct of all users interacting with the BookHive website and its services.

2. Privacy

BookHive respects the privacy of its users. We collect and process personal information in accordance with our Privacy Policy. By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in the Privacy Policy.

3. Community Guidelines

We strive to maintain a respectful and inclusive community on BookHive. Users are expected to engage in civil discourse and refrain from posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content. Violation of these guidelines may result in account suspension or termination.

4. Intellectual Property

BookHive respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects its users to do the same. Users must not post or distribute copyrighted material without proper authorization. Any infringement of intellectual property rights will be promptly addressed.

5. Security

We prioritize the security of our users' information and employ industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of data. However, users are responsible for safeguarding their login credentials and should report any suspected security breaches immediately.

6. Compliance

Users must comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and contractual obligations when using BookHive. Any illegal or fraudulent activity will result in account suspension or termination, and may be reported to the relevant authorities.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Ethics and Policy, please contact us at support@bookhive.com.
